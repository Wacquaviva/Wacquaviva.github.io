Shipwrecked Blossoms

Culturally inpsired creative design project.

My product is a representation of the life after death of sunken boats. In my coastal town, fishing has a huge market, and many go on boats for enjoyment as well. Due to this, accidents happen and sometimes, boats sink, or more commonly crash. While this marks the death of the boat, it also marks the beginning of a new habitat for wildlife including fish and plants at the sea floor. To represent this, I made a flower vase consisting of 2 halves of a boat, a base, and a lobster trap buoy. I will incorporate Natural elements by filling the base with sand, using real rocks to create a fake Jetty in the center, and including shells and sea life that can survive and provide nutrients for the plants, prolonging the life of the flowers held inside the vase.
